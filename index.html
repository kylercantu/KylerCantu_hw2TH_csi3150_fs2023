About me

Hello current reader(s), my name is Kyler and I'm from Troy, Michigan! I'm currently attending Oakland University and pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Prior to majoring in Computer Science, I spent about two and a half years pursuing an entirely different degree, which was Criminal Justice. I come from a Law Enforcement family and always thought that's what I wanted to do in the future, but at the same time I was always the kid who loved playing with technology/electronics. I've always heard about coding/programming growing up but never thought that I could do it since I had zero experience and never wrote a single line of code once in my life. Fast forward to my college years, while still pursuing my Criminal Justice Degree, I decided to attend an Intro to Coding Workshop at Grand Circus in Detroit which was a one-day two hour session where they taught the very basics of the java programming language. Ever since I wrote that first line of code, I knew that this is what I wanted to do!


My short term goal while I'm still a student is to get internships so that I can gain knowledge and understand how the industry works in the real world. For my long term goal in this profession, as of right now, I would like to hopefully become a back-end engineer! To know more about me, click on my Resume page and/or Fun Facts page!
